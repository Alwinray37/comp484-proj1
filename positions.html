Positions in Volleyball

  • Setter: The setter is responsible for setting up the ball for the attackers. They are often considered the team's playmaker.
  • Outside Hitter: The outside hitter is typically the primary attacker on the left side of the court.
  • Middle Blocker: The middle blocker is responsible for blocking shots and attacking from the center of the court.
  • Libero: The libero is a defensive specialist who wears a different colored jersey and can substitute freely for any back-row player.
